About

The love for guinea pigs across the globe has finally hit rock bottom and the enslavement of mankind by the Cat Overlords is absolute. Stripped of their cosy man-made homes and forced into hiding, the guinea pigs have turned on each other, battling for scarce resources to build their shelters anew.

As leader of your guinea pig tribe, it’s up to you to make the decisions that will ensure the survival of your fellow piggies.

Fight for resources, build shelters, pray for miracles, capture prisoners, house the wounded and protect your Forgotten Floof from catastrophe.

When will I get my floof?

We have a number of quotes from manufacturers and fulfilment partners who have estimated the end of November 2018 for final delivery but to be on the safe side we'd rather under promise and over deliver so we're aiming to have your Floof with you by the end of January 2019.

Risks and challenges

Though this is our first foray into the world of table top gaming we have a collective 25 years of experience in delivering large scale projects.

Coupled with a number of partners that have a lot of experience in this area we're already well on our way to making good on the promises of the campaign.

We're not doing complicated rewards or anything that would make the distribution process take longer and by keeping things as simple as possible we're reducing the risk on the project.

We want you to be able to get your hands on some guinea-floof as quickly as possible and we're taking the steps necessary to make this happen.
